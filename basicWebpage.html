Blog writing is the process of creating content for an online journal or informational website known as a blog. This content typically includes articles, diary-style entries, or opinion pieces that are regularly updated and written in an engaging, conversational style. The primary aim of blog writing is to inform, entertain, or engage readers on various topics, ranging from personal experiences to industry-specific news and advice.Blog Writing Format

The copyright symbol - the letter C in a circle - is a universally accepted symbol for copyright. To copyright your material, you can use this symbol © or use the word “Copyright.” However, most people choose to go with the symbol © as it is highly recognizable and is concise.
